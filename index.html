Biography

  • Born in 1959.
  • Joined The Industrial Bank of Japan (now Mizuho Bank) in 1984.
  • In 1995 at IBJ Securities, secured the bookrunner mandate for Kansai Electric Power’s corporate bond—the first such mandate for a bank-affiliated securities house—opening the door to the power bond market and subsequent gains in Japan’s bond underwriting league tables. [Ref-1]
  • Appointed Executive Officer, Head of Global Capital Markets, Mizuho Securities (2012).
  • In 2015, contributed to Mizuho’s acquisition of RBS’s North American loan assets, expanding the US DCM franchise. [Ref-2]
  • Also in 2015, became Managing Executive Officer at Mizuho Financial Group and Mizuho Securities (Domestic Sales / IPO oversight).
  • No.1 by IPO lead mandates in H1 2016 (KPMG). [Ref-3]
  • Senior Managing Executive Officer, Head of Global Investment Banking (2017–2021). From FY2018 to FY2021, Mizuho ranked No.1 in Japan’s public/straight bond underwriting and received IFR “Yen Bond House of the Year”. SDGs/ESG bonds: No.1 in Japan for FY2019–FY2021. [Ref-4,5]
  • Director, Mizuho Securities USA (from 2018).
  • Led a strategic global M&A alliance with Perella Weinberg Partners (2019). [Ref-6]
  • Japan Post share sale (3rd tranche): selected as Global Coordinator by Japan’s MoF in 2019; executed in 2021. [Ref-7]
  • Vice President & Executive Officer, Mizuho Securities (2021) [Ref-13]; Director in April 2022; stepped down in June 2022.
  • Jul 2022, appointed Director (Audit & Supervisory Committee Member), Simplex Holdings, Inc. (current).
  • Nov 2022, Executive Advisor, Axil Capital Advisors (current).
  • Apr 2023, Executive Advisor, Strategy Advisors Inc. (current).
