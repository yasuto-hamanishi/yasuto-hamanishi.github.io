Biography

  • Born in 1959.
  • Joined The Industrial Bank of Japan (now Mizuho Bank) in 1984.
  • In 1995 at IBJ Securities, led the first-ever mandate by a bank-affiliated securities firm as bookrunner for Kansai Electric Power’s corporate bond, marking entry into the power bond market and paving the way for later league-table gains in Japan’s bond underwriting. [Ref-1]
  • Appointed Head of Global Capital Markets (Executive Officer), Mizuho Securities in 2012.
  • In 2015, involved in Mizuho’s acquisition of RBS’s North American loan assets, expanding the US DCM franchise. [Ref-2]
  • Also in 2015, became Managing Executive Officer at Mizuho Financial Group and Managing Executive Officer at Mizuho Securities (Domestic Sales / IPO oversight).
  • In H1 2016, Mizuho Securities ranked No.1 by number of IPO lead mandates (KPMG). [Ref-3]
  • Head of Global Investment Banking (Senior Managing Executive Officer), 2017–2021. From FY2018–FY2021, Mizuho led Japan’s public/straight bond underwriting and received IFR’s “Yen Bond House of the Year”. SDGs/ESG bonds: No.1 in Japan for FY2019–FY2021. [Ref-4, Ref-5]
  • Director, Mizuho Securities USA (from 2018).
  • In 2019, led a strategic global M&A alliance with Perella Weinberg Partners. [Ref-6]
  • Japan Post share sale (3rd tranche): Ministry of Finance selected Mizuho as Global Coordinator (with others) in 2019; transaction executed in 2021. [Ref-7]
  • Vice President & Executive Officer, Mizuho Securities in 2021; Director in April 2022; stepped down in June 2022.
